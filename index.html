About Me

I am a research assistant at York University. I received my M.Sc in Computer Science from the University of Manitoba where I was advised by Dr. Yang Wang. My current interests encompass applied machine learning various topics including camera pose, 2D/3D scene understanding, instance segmentation, and object detection.
